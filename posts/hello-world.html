Hello World

Beg2DFIR.

Beg to differ, even.

A statement of disagreement. An objection to fate. Contesting nature’s way of entropy and decay, we can still insist that we are growing, and that we still have more to give.

We are not dying. We are transforming.

Even though we are starting over, this is not exactly ex nihilo — not from nothing.

My first real doorway into the field was remote help desk work. Since then, I have touched different parts of tech through work, study, troubleshooting, and small experiments. The pieces were real, but they were scattered. Beg2DFIR is where I start organizing them into a deliberate path.

Beg2DFIR will not just serve as a container for tech information. It will live as proof of the transformations we are capable of. Proof that scattered experience, old knowledge, practical exposure, and small experiments can still be shaped into something worth building.

This site is where I will collect the useful parts of that process: posts, videos, study tools, projects, and proof that the path is being walked.

The goal is to build in the open: clear enough for beginners to follow, useful enough for others to study from, and concrete enough to show the work taking shape.

Over time, some of that work may become apps, games, tools, or something I cannot fully see yet.

For now, it starts here: developing what remains into the next horizon of knowledge and ability.
